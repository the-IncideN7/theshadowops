The AdHash heatmap

The AdHash heatmap enables you to track each click of your advertising campaign. With exact coordinates and four different resolutions down to the pixel, your heatmaps will never be the same. It was really a challenge to adjust the sizes and colors, but with the help of the team, we managed to release a beautiful and functional product to our clients.
