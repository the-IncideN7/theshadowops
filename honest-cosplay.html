Honest Cosplay logo

This logo was one of my biggest challenges at the time. The wing is an update of one of my old design ideas that was never implemented. But it found home with Hones Cosplay. We've tried various options, including two wings. One angelic, one devilish. But somehow, one wing was more appealing.

Honest Cosplay Business card

This business cards are filled with nerdiness (if such a word even exists). I wanted to make the design unique, but not confusing. Everyone has a title, like the one shown. Paladin. Overlord. Assassin. Bard. etc. And when you project starts with "Honest", your team must be too. So I incorporated the logo on the left with the title on the right. The background has a scratched metal texture. Sadly it's hard to see. It was a headache to adjust the colors, but it was worth it. I usually don't do print, but this one I am proud of.
